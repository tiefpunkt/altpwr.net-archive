Materials we need

Some people, hackerspaces or companies might just have lying around what we need. This helps in saving money for the rest of the project. And re-using materials is always better.

  • 12 pieces of 30m, 5x6mm2 flexible cable (H07RN-F or AT-N07V3V3-F)
  • 30+ MOSFETs for the overvoltage crowbars (60V, 200A TO-247, SOT-227 B or bigger)
  • One or two HP ESP120 power supplies. (We already have the six we need, but one is damaged on the outside and another has the internal fan stuck on full speed.)
